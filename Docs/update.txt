DragonPicoSD firmware update instructions.

Things you will need :
	A modern PC running Windows, Linux or MacOS.
	A USB cable with a micro usb connector on one end.
	
Procedure :
	Power down your Dragon/CoCo and unplug the cartridge.
	Remove the top cover of the cartridge by removing the 4 crosshead screws on the bottom.**
	Plug the micro usb end of the connector into the connector on the pi-pico.
	Plug the other end of the lead into your PC.
	Hold down the white BOOTSEL button on the pi-pico, keep this held down and press the
		reset pico button on the DragonPicoSD board.
	The Pico should show up as a drive in your OS.
	Copy the dragonpicosd.uf2 file to this drive (drag and drop from GUI, or copy/cp from command line).
	The pico drive should now disconnect and the firmware will have been updated.
	Re-connect the DragonPicoSD to your Dragon/CoCo.
	Power on the system and check the firmware virsion with the helpx command.

**The test units don't have a top case so skip this step....
